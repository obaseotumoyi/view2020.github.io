Welcome to AIESEC in Nigeria Virtual Education Week. Your first ever AIESEC in Nigeria conference, are you ready to lead from home.

ViEW 2020 is a 3-day virtual conference that engages members of AIESEC in Nigeria and focuses on developing the capacity needed for the current context. VIEW features topics like:

Working Remotely

After this pandemic, remote working would become very popular in the labour market. Getting used to remote learning is the best way to prepare yourself for that world. It starts with ViEW 2020.

Understand your role in AIESEC

Do you know why we do what we do? Do you see how your role leads to your development and the development of others? Attend the Virtual Education Week to learn more about your function in AIESEC.
